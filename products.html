Interior coatings

PhotoDeco

Decorative interior wall paint, wide colour range. Self-cleaning, helps reduce indoor airborne pollutants, low-VOC.

PhotoWall

Breathable coating for interior walls and ceilings where moisture management matters. Self-cleaning, low-VOC.

PhotoLacq

Invisible treatments & infrastructure

PhotoActiva S

Invisible treatment for exposed and coloured surfaces. ≈10–15 m²/L; maintenance-free, permanent effect.

PhotoActiva 130

Treatment for non-porous indoor surfaces. ≈20 m²/L; activates even at very low light levels.

PhotoCryl (Asphalt)

Photocatalytic treatment for roads, car parks and motorways. NOx breakdown measured at 286 µg NO/m²·h in tests.

PhotoTunnel

White tunnel-lining coating that stays bright and breaks down airborne pollutants.
