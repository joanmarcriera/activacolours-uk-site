The laboratory evidence behind the claims

Every claim we make in the UK maps to a recognised test method. Ask us for the certificates relevant to your project.

ISO 27448 — self-cleaning

The standard test for photocatalytic self-cleaning performance of a surface. This is the evidence behind "keeps surfaces cleaner for longer".

ISO 22197-1 — NOx removal

The standard test for removal of nitrogen oxides by photocatalytic materials. Manufacturer testing of the invisible treatments in this range has been carried out at independent laboratories, including CSIC (the Spanish national research council).

Claims: ActivaColours coatings are supplied in the UK as self-cleaning, pollutant/NOx-reducing and low-VOC products only. Project references above describe where the products have been used and how they were tested; performance for your project should be confirmed against the relevant certificates before specification.
