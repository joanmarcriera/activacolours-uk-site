Sales agents — UK regions

Sell something the other reps can't

We're building a network of self-employed, commission-only sales agents across the UK to introduce ActivaColours photocatalytic coatings to the trade. Your network and your hours; our product, our documentation, our leads.

The opportunity

ActivaColours UK supplies self-cleaning, pollutant-reducing, low-VOC coatings to the trade by the pallet — a genuinely differentiated line in a market of me-too paints, backed by ISO test methods and more than a decade of European installations (see the track record). We are opening UK regions to agents who already know the buyers: builders' merchants, contractors, facilities and estates teams, housing providers, architects and specifiers.

Uncapped commission

You earn a commission on every sale you bring that gets paid — no cap, no thresholds to unlock. Terms are agreed at onboarding. Typical orders are pallet-scale, so single sales are meaningful.

Your own time

No fixed hours, no quotas, no exclusivity demanded. Keep your existing work and add ActivaColours to your bag. You are self-employed and invoice us for your commission.

We do the heavy lifting

We provide the full sales pack — datasheets, ISO test data, sell sheets, a product training call and pricing support — plus enquiries from our national LinkedIn campaign routed to your region. You open the door; we quote, supply and invoice.

Who we're looking for

People with a live network in one or more of: builders' merchants and trade counters, façade and render contractors, painters and decorators, facilities management and estates, social housing, architecture and specification. B2B field-sales experience helps; knowing the buyers matters more. You'll need to be UK-based and able to work on a self-employed basis.
